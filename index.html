A Short Synopsis Regarding Quotes

Although quotes, such as the one above, may seem cliche at first glace, a further examination into the impact of quotes on our daily lives reveals the significance of quotes and, at the very least, shows how quotes can be utilized to bring a smile to the reader's face or a short pondering of whether or not a quote contains any truth. A good quote can really sell a literture piece or product. For example, a quote written by Albert Einstein sold in an auction house in Jerusalem for $1.56 million dollars. The quote was a one sentence piece of advice on happy living stating "a calm and humble life will bring more happiness than the pursuit of success and the constant restlessness that comes with it." This illustrates the value of quotes whether it be monetary or not. Furthermore, a second note from Albert Einstein reading "where there's a will, there's a way" sold for $240,000. Besides monetary value, quotes used in research papers or articles can effectively and directly communicate an writier's idea in a way that an entire essay may fail to do so. Aside selling quotes and utlizing them in a paper, quotes can be the fuel for motivation. Many people turn to quotes when they feel low on motivaton in hopes of seeing a quote that rekindles their motivation.
